# Privacy Policy — On Command

**Effective Date:** April 14, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to On Command. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our macOS application On Command. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

By downloading or using On Command, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Age Requirement

On Command is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly provide services to children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this App.

---

## 3. Information We Collect

On Command does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. Specifically, we do not collect:

- Personal identification information (such as name, email address, or phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage data or analytics
- Financial or payment information
- Any other personally identifiable information

The only data stored by the App is your all-time high score, which is saved **locally on your device** using standard system storage. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible by us or any third party.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

On Command does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external services that collect user data. We do not share any information with third parties because no such information is collected.

---

## 5. Data Security

Since On Command does not collect any personal data, there is no user data stored on our servers or systems. Your use of the App remains entirely private. The local high score data stored on your device is managed by your operating system and is subject to macOS's built-in security protections.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

On Command is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used our App, please contact us at the email address below, and we will take appropriate action.
